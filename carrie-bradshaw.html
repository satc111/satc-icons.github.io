Carrie Bradshaw

Carrie Bradshaw's fashion style is a bold and eclectic mix of high fashion, vintage pieces, and an unapologetic love for all things glamorous, with her wardrobe often reflecting her personality — chic, playful, and fiercely independent. One of her most iconic fashion obsessions is her collection of Manolo Blahnik shoes, which became a symbol of both her sophisticated taste and her unyielding belief that the perfect pair of shoes could elevate any outfit, as well as her love for luxury and the finer things in life. Whether paired with a delicate tutu, a tailored suit, or her signature Cosmopolitan in hand, Carrie’s devotion to Manolos transcended mere footwear — they became an extension of her character, symbolizing confidence, elegance, and a touch of her romantic side.

Fashion inspired by the iconic characters of 'Sex and the City'.
